5. Asso. Research Manager Incentives

Associate Research Managers are managers that also are involved in delivering TRRs. Their combined tasks and incentives are based on team productivity and also deliver recruits.

Asso. Research Managers Incentives (managing are 2 or fewer researchers are):

1. Research Incentives: Aligned with Research Analyst Incentives

2. RM Incentives: INR 6,000/- per team member

Asso. Research Managers Incentives (managing are 3 to 4 researchers are):

3. Research Incentives: Aligned with Research Associate Incentives

4. RM Incentives: INR 6,000/- per team member


6. Asso. Account Manager Incentives

Associate Account Managers are managers that also are involved in delivering TRRs. Their combined tasks and incentives are based on client management and also deliver recruits

Asso. Account Managers Incentives (managing 1 client is):

1. Research Incentives: Aligned with Research Analyst Incentives

2. AM Incentives: Aligned with AAM Incentives

Asso. Account Managers Incentives (managing 2 to 4 clients are):

3. Research Incentives: Aligned with Research Associate Incentives

4. AM Incentives: Aligned with AAM Incentives

**If reporting to KAM, KAM will get incentivized on the profit / GP / revenue/conversion of AAM’s clients (currently none reporting to KAM)
